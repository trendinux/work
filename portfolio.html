Loopwork — Agentic AI Operations Console

Designing the supervisory layer that lets ops teams trust, steer, and audit a fleet of autonomous AI agents — without reading a single log file.

As AI agents move from answering questions to actually executing multi-step business workflows — processing refunds, scheduling vendors, triaging support — the people supervising them need a different kind of dashboard. Not another chart. A trust interface.

Loopwork is a self-directed concept project exploring that problem: how do you make an autonomous system's reasoning visible enough that a non-technical operator can intervene at exactly the right moment, and never has to wonder what an agent did or why?

  • Plain-language activity feed — every agent step described in the vocabulary of the task, not the system
  • Live confidence & risk scoring, so low-certainty actions surface for review automatically
  • One-tap approve / escalate queue for any action above a risk threshold
  • Full replay and audit trail for every completed workflow, end to end

03 HOW I WORK

A repeatable process for fast-moving teams

The medium changes — storefront, dashboard, or agent console — the sequence doesn't.

01

Discover

Stakeholder interviews, analytics review, and a competitive audit to find where the real friction is.

02

Define

User flows and information architecture, then low-fidelity wireframes to test the structure cheaply.

03

Design

High-fidelity UI and a reusable design system, built in Figma with engineering in the room.

04

Validate

Usability and A/B testing against real users before anything ships to production.

05

Ship & measure

Clean handoff, instrumented analytics, and a feedback loop back into the next iteration.
